How to Reach Indore

By Air

Devi Ahilyabai Holkar Airport in Indore is located 10 km from the city centre. It is well connected by flights to Indian cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Bhopal, Jaipur, etc. From the airport, taxi services are available.

By Train

Indore is an important railway station. Trains from Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Pune, Malwa, Delhi and other cities halt at the station.
Railway Station(s): Indore Junction Bg (INDB), Lakshmibai Nagar (LMNR), Rajendra Nagar (RJQ), Indore Junction Mg (INDM), Lokmanya Nagar (LKMN), Fatehabad Chowk Junction (FTD)

By Road

Indore is well connected by road. State run and private buses from Mumbai, Pune, Bhopal, Ujjain and other cities of Madhya Pradesh ply regularly to Indore.
